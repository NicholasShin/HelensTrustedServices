Tutoring✏️

I graduated from Western Washington University with a Bachelor’s of Science in Applied Mathematics. During my time there, I volunteered in foreign exchange programs-teaching international students English, and mentored low-income/BIPOC students in Skagit county through Compass2Campus. I have always had a passion for helping others; which led me into working in education with English Language Learners and students with disabilities. I have experience tutoring K-12 and community college students.

I choose to tutor because I believe in the innate abilities all students hold. Every student brings different experiences and backgrounds to the table that guide their unique educational journey. I give students the space to teach me how they learn so I can effectively teach them and build their confidence.

Subjects Offered

  • High School Algebra & Geometry
  • Pre-Calculus & Trigonometry
  • AP Calculus (AB/BC)
  • Linear Algebra & Differential Equations
  • Statistics & Data Analysis
