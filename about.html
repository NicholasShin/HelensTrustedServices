About Me

I graduated from Western Washington University with a Bachelor’s of Science in Applied Mathematics. During my time there, I volunteered in the community. I helped teach international students English, and mentored low-income/BIPOC students in Skagit county through Compass2Campus. Additionally, I work in education with English Language Learners and students with disabilities. I have experience tutoring K-12 and community college students.

My goal is to help others and make a positive impact. My patience and adaptability help me effectively teach students from diverse backgrounds and abilities.

I believe the key to student success is balancing learning a new skillset and developing the mindset for perseverance and success. Identifying students’ strengths, reframing their learning style in a positive light, and building confidence are crucial to students’ academic success.
